Ariadne Ellis is a versatile actress and model, acclaimed for breathing life into her characters onscreen and a favorite among Fashion Week designers worldwide. Hailed as "a true breakout" for her most recent role in Running Behind, Ariadne now splits her time between the runway and the film set. Upcoming projects include Paris Fashion Week and a scripted series in L.A.
